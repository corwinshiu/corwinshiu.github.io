I am a Ph.D. candidate in Physics advised by Bill Jones. My primarly research work is in experimental cosmology studying the cosmic microwave background. The bulk of my thesis work has been with the SPIDER collaboration, a balloon borne CMB polarimeter, and I have been fortunate to be involved in all aspects CMB: detector design, instrument testing and integration, and analysis. I am part of the integration team of SPIDER II and deployed for the upcoming 2022/2023 Antarctic season.

More generally I am interested in all things involving systems engineering and getting good data from machines: from designing and building better instruments to developing statistical techniques to extract information from noisy instruments.
